Plant Disease Detection using a Channel Weighting Layer (CWL) integrated with ConvNeXt-Tiny

Ongoing — M.Tech Thesis
Supervisor: Dr. Amit Prakash Singh · USICT, Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University

A novel input-level RGB channel weighting mechanism — using softmax-normalized, learnable weights — applied ahead of a ConvNeXt-Tiny backbone for plant disease classification. The work includes a systematic ablation study and two-level explainability, combining CWL spectral channel weights with Grad-CAM visualizations to interpret model behaviour at both the channel and pixel level.
